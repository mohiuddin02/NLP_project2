LONDON, England — British actor Alan Rickman has died. He was 69 years old.

<img class="pull-right" src="https://newsela-test-files-f331e.s3.amazonaws.com/article_media/extra/Obit_Alan_Rickman_Edit.png"/>

Alan acted in movies and plays. He was very good at playing bad guys. He played professor Severus Snape in the "Harry Potter" movies. That was one of his biggest parts.

## "Harry Potter" Movies

Daniel Radcliffe played the part of Harry Potter, the young wizard.

Daniel said Rickman was a great actor. He was "one of the greatest actors I will ever work with." The two were in eight "Harry Potter" movies together. 

Alan was born in London in 1946. He died there early Thursday. 

## What A Good Bad Guy!

Alan learned to act at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art. He liked playing bad people. People had fun watching him play bad guys.

Alan did not only play bad people. He played brave and funny people too.

Alan had big parts in many movies. He played in "Die Hard" and "Truly Madly Deeply." He was in "Robin Hood: Prince of Thieves" and "Love Actually." 

Alan had big parts in many plays too. He was talented in many ways.

## Professor Of Magic

His biggest part was as Snape in the "Potter" movies. 

Snape is a professor of magic at the Hogwarts school. Viewers are not always sure if he is good or bad. Sometimes he seems to want to hurt Harry. Other times he seems to want to help him. 

## Helping A Young Actor

Daniel was very young when he started making the "Potter" movies. Alan helped him to get better at acting. 

Daniel said he will always remember what Rickman told him. It will help make him a better actor "for the rest of my life." 

J.K. Rowling is the writer of the "Harry Potter" books. She said she is very sad Rickman has died. He was a great actor, she said. He was "a wonderful man" too.
